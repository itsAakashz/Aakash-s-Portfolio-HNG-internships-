My goals for the next two years in the tech field.

Gain a thorough understanding of fundamental technologies such as operating systems, networking, and databases.
Action Plan: Complete online courses and certifications, and build and experiment with small projects to understand how these systems work.

2. Master New Programming Languages and Frameworks
Learn and become proficient in at least three new programming languages and their associated frameworks.
Action Plan: Dedicate time each week to studying and practicing new languages, and develop small projects or contribute to open-source projects using these new languages.

3. Work on Innovative Projects
Create innovative projects that solve real-world problems and contribute to society.
Action Plan: Identify problems that need technological solutions, collaborate with like-minded individuals to brainstorm and develop these projects, and share progress and get feedback from the tech community.

4. Focus on Sustainable Technology
Use my tech skills to create solutions that benefit the environment and promote sustainability.
Action Plan: Research and stay updated on the latest in green technology, develop projects that reduce waste, improve energy efficiency, or otherwise help the environment, and partner with organizations focused on sustainability to gain insights and contribute meaningfully.

5. Engage with the Tech Community
Become an active member of the tech community to learn from others, share knowledge, and collaborate on projects.
Action Plan: Attend tech meetups, conferences, and workshops, participate in online forums, contribute to discussions, and write blogs about my learnings, mentor newcomers and share my journey to inspire others.

6. Build a Personal Brand
Establish myself as a knowledgeable and innovative tech enthusiast.
Action Plan: Regularly update my personal website with my projects, learnings, and blog posts, leverage social media to share my work and connect with other tech enthusiasts, and speak at events or webinars to share my insights and experiences.

7. Explore Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

8. Contribute to Open Source
Give back to the tech community by contributing to open-source projects.
Action Plan: Identify open-source projects that align with my interests and skills, regularly contribute code, documentation, or other resources, and engage with the open-source community to learn and collaborate.

9. Develop Soft Skills
Improve my communication, teamwork, and leadership skills to enhance my professional growth.
Action Plan: Take part in team projects, both in academic and professional settings, seek feedback and work on areas of improvement, and attend workshops or courses focused on soft skills development.

10. Stay Curious and Keep Learning
Maintain my curiosity and passion for technology by continuously learning and exploring new areas.
Action Plan: Set aside time each week for learning and experimentation, stay updated with tech news, trends, and advancements, and never hesitate to dive deep into new and unfamiliar topics.
